# Privacy Policy for reWrite

**Effective date:** 14 July 2026  
**Last updated:** 14 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **reWrite**, operated by **Joshua Tan** in Singapore (“reWrite,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use:

- the reWrite desktop application for macOS or Windows (the “App”); and
- the reWrite website at [rewriteai.dev](https://rewriteai.dev) (the “Website”).

By using reWrite, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account information

When you create a reWrite account, we may collect:

- your name;
- your email address;
- authentication identifiers and tokens;
- your account settings; and
- your subscription plan and account status.

We use Supabase to provide account authentication and related backend services.

### 1.2 Text submitted for rewriting

When you request a rewrite, the App processes:

- the text you highlighted or selected; and
- the rewrite instruction or option you selected.

This information is transmitted to Anthropic so that its AI service can generate the requested rewrite. The generated response is then returned to the App.

reWrite does **not** intentionally store the highlighted text, rewrite instruction, or generated response on reWrite-operated servers as part of the normal rewriting process. However, Anthropic may retain API inputs and outputs in accordance with its own policies, as explained in Section 4.

### 1.3 Information accessed on your device

To provide its features, the App may request or use operating-system permissions that allow it to access:

- accessibility features;
- your clipboard;
- selected or highlighted text;
- the name of the active application;
- the active window title;
- global keyboard shortcuts or hotkeys;
- screen content or screenshots; and
- information needed to insert rewritten text back into the active application.

These permissions are used to detect the context in which reWrite is activated and to perform the action you request.

Unless otherwise stated in this Privacy Policy, reWrite sends only the highlighted text and the rewrite instruction to Anthropic. reWrite does not intentionally send full screenshots, your entire screen, unrelated clipboard contents, or the contents of other applications to Anthropic.

reWrite does not request access to your microphone or to general files and folders for its normal rewriting functionality.

### 1.4 Information stored locally

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

- custom prompts; and
- rewrite history.

Local information remains on your device unless it is included in information you deliberately send to support, is captured in a diagnostic report, or is otherwise described in this Privacy Policy.

### 1.5 Diagnostics, logs, and usage information

We may collect technical and usage information necessary to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve reWrite, including:

- IP address;
- device type;
- operating-system type and version;
- App or browser version;
- crash reports;
- diagnostic and performance logs;
- dates and times of access;
- feature-usage information;
- error information; and
- security and fraud-prevention information.

Diagnostic information may occasionally include limited technical context associated with an error. We do not intend for diagnostic logs to collect the full contents of your documents or communications.

### 1.6 Payment and subscription information

Payments are processed by Stripe. reWrite does not directly collect or store your full payment-card number, card security code, or complete banking credentials.

Stripe may collect billing and payment information directly from you. reWrite may receive limited information needed to manage your subscription, such as:

- a Stripe customer or transaction identifier;
- your selected plan;
- subscription and payment status;
- purchase and renewal dates; and
- other non-sensitive transaction information.

Stripe handles payment information under its own terms and privacy policy.

### 1.7 Website and contact information

When you visit the Website or submit a contact form, we may collect:

- your name;
- your email address;
- the contents of your message;
- any files or screenshots you choose to attach;
- your IP address;
- browser and device information; and
- website security, request, and diagnostic logs.

Cloudflare may also process network, browser, IP-address, cookie, and security information when it provides website delivery, security, and related services.

### 1.8 Support communications

When you contact us for support, we may retain:

- your email address;
- your name;
- your support messages;
- screenshots or recordings you voluntarily provide;
- diagnostic files you voluntarily provide; and
- information about how the issue was resolved.

Please avoid including passwords, payment-card details, confidential documents, or other unnecessary sensitive information in support messages.

## 2. How We Use Information

We may use information to:

- create, authenticate, and manage your account;
- provide AI-powered rewriting features;
- return rewritten text to the application you are using;
- save your local preferences, custom prompts, and rewrite history;
- process subscriptions and confirm payment status;
- provide customer support;
- diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues;
- understand how features are used and improve reWrite;
- protect reWrite, our users, and our service providers from fraud, abuse, and security threats;

- enforce our terms and protect our legal rights; and
- comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, and lawful government requests.

We do not sell your highlighted text or use it for targeted advertising.

## 3. Legal Bases for Processing

Where laws such as the European Union General Data Protection Regulation or United Kingdom GDPR apply, we process personal information under one or more of the following legal bases:

- **Performance of a contract:** to create your account, provide rewriting features, and manage your subscription.
- **Legitimate interests:** to secure, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve reWrite, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights.
- **Consent:** where you grant device permissions, submit optional information, or where consent is otherwise required.
- **Legal obligations:** to comply with tax, accounting, regulatory, security, and other legal requirements.

You may withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. Withdrawal does not affect processing that occurred before consent was withdrawn.

## 4. AI Processing by Anthropic

reWrite uses Anthropic’s API to generate rewrites.

When you activate a rewrite, reWrite sends Anthropic:

- the highlighted text; and
- the selected rewrite instruction.

Anthropic processes this information and returns an AI-generated response.

As of the effective date of this Privacy Policy, Anthropic states that:

- inputs and outputs submitted through its standard commercial API are generally deleted from its backend within 30 days, subject to exceptions such as legal requirements, usage-policy enforcement, longer-retention features, or a separate retention agreement; and
- inputs and outputs from commercial products, including the Anthropic API, are not used to train Anthropic’s models by default.

Anthropic’s practices may change, and its processing is governed by its own policies. You should review:

- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy)
- [Anthropic API data-retention information](https://privacy.anthropic.com/en/articles/7996866-how-long-do-you-store-personal-data)
- [Anthropic information about model training](https://privacy.anthropic.com/en/articles/7996868-is-my-data-used-for-model-training)

### Sensitive information warning

Because highlighted text is transmitted to a third-party AI provider, do not use reWrite to process information that you are not authorised to disclose.

You should avoid submitting:

- passwords, authentication codes, or security keys;
- full payment-card or banking details;
- government identification numbers;
- confidential business information or trade secrets;
- private health, medical, legal, or financial records;
- personal information about another person without permission; or
- information subject to contractual, professional, regulatory, or legal confidentiality restrictions.

You are responsible for ensuring that you have the right to submit the text you ask reWrite to process.
